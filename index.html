About me

My name is Thamizhventhan Kannadas, and I am from Jaffna, Sri Lanka. I started my journey towards becoming a full-stack developer when I was only 11 years old and I am currently 15 years old. I am a self-taught web developer and I have gained expertise in developing websites. I am currently studying in the 11th grade.

My main goal as a web developer is to continuously educate myself on the latest advancements and trends in the industry. I find joy in tackling intricate projects and exploring the possibilities of web development. If you have a project that you believe suits my skills, I'd be enthusiastic to learn more about it.

In my opinion, each website ought to be one-of-a-kind and custom-made to meet the specific requirements of its intended audience. This is why I center my work around my clients, taking into account their business objectives and long-term vision. I collaborate closely with my clients to guarantee that the website I produce not only satisfies their expectations but also matches their preferences and needs.

Whether you require a basic website, an e-commerce platform, or a sophisticated web application, I possess the technical proficiency and expertise to deliver high-quality websites that are quick to respond, easily accessible, and user-friendly for everyone.
